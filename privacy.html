Privacy Policy Last updated: April 11, 2026 FORGE: Level Your Life ("FORGE", "we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, where it is stored, with whom it is shared, and the rights you have over your data when you use our iOS application. By using FORGE, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app. 1. Information We Collect 1.1 Account Data (via Sign in with Apple) Name — optional, only if you choose to share it via Sign in with Apple. Email address — optional; you may use Apple's Hide My Email feature, in which case we only receive an anonymous private relay address. Firebase Authentication User ID (UID) — a unique anonymized identifier generated by Firebase to recognize your account across devices. 1.2 Game Progress & Character Data Username, character level, experience points (XP), Gold, Gems Six character attributes (Strength, Intellect, Discipline, Charisma, Endurance, Wisdom) Streaks, Forge Heat value, Badges earned, Visions completed Cosmetic items owned (profile frames, title colors, themes) 1.3 Productivity Data Habits, Dailies, and To-Dos you create Task completion statistics and history Focus Timer sessions and total focus minutes 1.4 Technical Data Device identifiers used by Google AdMob to serve advertisements (free tier only). You can opt out of personalized ads in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising. Subscription receipts processed by Apple. We never see or store your payment information. 2. Why We Collect This Data Core app functionality — to provide the FORGE experience (task management, character progression, gamified rewards). Cloud sync & backup — to synchronize your progress across multiple devices and protect against data loss. Leaderboards & social features — to display rankings and friend lists if you choose to participate. Anti-cheat — to detect and prevent manipulation of leaderboard standings. Account management — authentication, account recovery, and account deletion. Notifications — to send optional reminders for habits and dailies (only if you enable them). Advertising — to display ads in the free version via Google AdMob. Pro subscribers see no ads. 3. Where Your Data Is Stored All account, character, and productivity data is stored on Google Firebase (Firestore database and Firebase Authentication), hosted in the europe-west6 region — Google Cloud's data center in Zürich, Switzerland. Data is protected by Firebase Security Rules and encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. 4. Who Sees Your Data 4.1 Other FORGE Players If you opt in to the leaderboard (see Section 6), the following information becomes visible to other players: Your username Your character level, XP, and rank Your current streak Cosmetic items you have equipped (profile frame, title color) No private data — email, name, task content, habit details, or productivity statistics — is ever visible to other players. 4.2 Service Providers Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Functions) — infrastructure provider. See Firebase Privacy. Google AdMob — for ads in the free version. See Google Privacy. Apple — for Sign in with Apple authentication and subscription processing. See Apple Privacy. 4.3 No Sale of Data We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. The only advertising in FORGE is served by Google AdMob to free-tier users, and you can disable personalized ads in iOS Settings. 5. Data Retention & Deletion You have full control over your data and can delete your account at any time: Open FORGE → Settings → Account → Delete Account Confirm deletion when prompted When you delete your account, the following happens immediately: All Firestore documents associated with your UID (character, tasks, habits, statistics, badges, visions) are permanently removed Your Firebase Authentication account is permanently removed All locally stored data on your device is wiped Your username is released and removed from the leaderboard This action is irreversible. We do not retain backups of deleted accounts. If you cannot access the app to perform the deletion, you may also request deletion by writing to mxf.business@gmx.ch from your registered email address. 6. Consent FORGE works fully offline. Cloud sync, leaderboards, and any data upload to our servers require your explicit, opt-in consent. You are never opted in by default. On first launch (and any time you can revisit this in Settings), FORGE shows a consent dialog asking whether you agree to: Sign in with Apple and create a Firebase account Upload your character progress and statistics to Firebase Firestore Display your username, level, XP, rank, and streak on the public leaderboard If you decline, FORGE remains fully usable in offline mode — all data stays on your device only. You can withdraw your consent at any time by deleting your account in Settings. 7. Children FORGE is rated 4+ in the App Store but is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us at mxf.business@gmx.ch and we will delete the account immediately. 8. Your Rights (GDPR / Swiss DSG) As FORGE is operated from Switzerland and uses servers in the EU/EEA, you have the following rights under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP/DSG): Right of access — request a copy of all data we hold about you Right of rectification — correct inaccurate data Right of erasure — delete your account and all associated data Right to data portability — request your data in a machine-readable format Right to object — opt out of leaderboard participation, notifications, and personalized ads at any time To exercise any of these rights, contact mxf.business@gmx.ch. 9. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Material changes will be communicated in-app. Continued use of FORGE after an update means you accept the revised policy. 10.
