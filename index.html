Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn XP and level up?

Complete tasks, habits, and dailies to earn XP. Each completed task also gives you attribute points based on the task category and difficulty.

What is Forge Heat?

Forge Heat tracks your daily activity. The more tasks you complete, the hotter your forge burns. Reaching 100% gives you a +5% Gold bonus the next day.

What is Rest Mode?

Rest Mode pauses your streaks and penalties for up to 3 days. After using it, there's a 14-day cooldown before you can activate it again.

How does the Focus Timer work?

The Focus Timer is a Pomodoro-style timer. Set your work and break durations, choose which attribute to train, and start forging. You earn 8 XP, 3 Gold, and 1 attribute point per completed session.

What are Visions?

Visions are your long-term goals. Break them down into milestones and track your progress. Completing milestones earns you XP and Gold.

How do I remove ads?

Subscribe to FORGE Pro from the Toolkit Settings section. This removes all advertisements and supports the continued development of FORGE.

Can I change my username?

Yes, but only once. Go to your Character profile and tap on your username to change it.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Toolkit, Settings, Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data. This action cannot be undone.
